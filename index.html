Intro

I earned my Ph.D in Applied and Computational Mathematics and Statistics (ACMS) department at the University of Notre Dame in 2023. My research focuses on recurrent neuronal network models and machine learning. I am also an enthusiastic educator and confident with many years of teaching experiences.

I received my M.S. in ACMS from Notre Dame in 2020 and B.A. in mathematics and statistics from University of California, Berkeley in 2018.

I also have diverse interests in life including: music, literature, movies, dance, and sports. I am an explorer, and I like to face all the challenges in life. Through the looking glass, I believed in a promising wonderland!


This is my CV, dissertation, and LinkedIn page.
